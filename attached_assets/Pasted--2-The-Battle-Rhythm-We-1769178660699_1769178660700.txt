  The goal of this schedule is to ensure information flows from the        
  PMO (Tactical) to the TMO (Operational) to the VRO (Strategic) in a      
  continuous loop.                                                         
                                                                           
  Day    Event    Purpose    Primary Owner                                 
  Monday    The Scrum of Scrums    Align the PMO teams on immediate        
  weekly roadblocks.    PMO Lead                                           
  Tuesday    Cross-Functional OPT    TMO & PMO meet to ensure the          
  roadmap is actually feasible based on Monday's updates.    TMO Lead      
  Wednesday    The Decision Node    High-level "Change Control." If a      
  project is failing, do we kill it?    VRO/Sponsors                       
  Thursday    The Value Pulse    VRO reviews data from the week to see     
  if we are still on track for the "End State."    VRO Lead                
  Friday    Orders for Next Week    Issue updated "intent" to all          
  teams based on the week’s discoveries.    Leadership                     
  3. The "Common Operational Picture" (Tooling)                            
  To make this work, you need a shared digital space (using tools like     
  Jira, Monday.com, or PowerBI) structured with these three layers:        
                                                                           
  Strategic Layer (VRO): A dashboard showing Value Realization. (e.g.,     
  "Are we saving the money we said we would?")                             
                                                                           
  Operational Layer (TMO): A visual Roadmap showing dependencies.          
  (e.g., "If Project A is late, how does it hurt Project B?")              
                                                                           
  Tactical Layer (PMO): A Task Board showing the current "ground           
  truth" of labor and effort.                                              
                                                                           
  How to Start the Transition                                              
  Instead of a massive reorganization, start with a "Rehearsal of          
  Concept" (ROC) Drill:                                                    
                                                                           
  Pick one high-priority initiative where the VRO, PMO, and TMO are        
  currently overlapping or conflicting.                                    
                                                                           
  Assign a single "Battle Captain" (a lead who has authority over all      
  three for just this project).                                            
                                                                           
  Run the Battle Rhythm for 30 days.
