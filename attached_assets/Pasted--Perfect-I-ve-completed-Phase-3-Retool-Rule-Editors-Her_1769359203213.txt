  ---                                                                                                                                               
  Integration Flow                                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                                                                    
  8 Retool Apps → PostgreSQL → AgentCollaborationRulesEngine → Deep Agents                                                                          
     (Configure)   (Store)     (Evaluate)                      (Execute)                                                                            
                                                                                                                                                    
  When teams update rules in Retool:                                                                                                                
  1. Rules saved to agent_collaboration_rules table                                                                                                 
  2. AgentCollaborationRulesEngine reads rules                                                                                                      
  3. When metrics trigger conditions, sends A2A messages                                                                                            
  4. Target agents execute actions                                                                                                                  
  5. Results logged to rule_execution_history                                                                                                       
  6. Retool shows execution history and stats
