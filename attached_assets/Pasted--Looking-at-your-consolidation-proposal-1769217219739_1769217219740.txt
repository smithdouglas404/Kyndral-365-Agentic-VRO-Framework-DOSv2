                                                           
● Looking at your consolidation proposal, I think the overall direction is 
  excellent - moving from 43 pages to 6-8 role-based workspaces will       
  dramatically improve navigation and cognitive load. Here are my thoughts:
                                                                           
  What Works Well                                                          
                                                                           
  1. Role-based segmentation is smart - Each workspace maps to a real job  
  function with clear responsibilities                                     
  2. Tab/panel approach - Using tabs within workspaces instead of separate 
  routes keeps context and reduces navigation jumps                        
  3. Reusing existing components - You're not rebuilding, just             
  reorganizing, which is efficient                                         
  4. Clear consolidation mapping - You've identified what pages fold into  
  each workspace                                                           
                                                                           
  Potential Concerns                                                       
                                                                           
  1. Multi-role users - Many users wear multiple hats (PM who also needs   
  budget view, Executive who wants to drill into project details).         
  Consider:                                                                
    - Allow users to have multiple role assignments                        
    - Quick role-switcher in the header                                    
    - Or a "My Workspace" that combines their assigned roles               
  2. Admin & Document Upload - You mentioned admin screens and document    
  uploading. Based on your table:                                          
    - Admin workspace handles users, roles, integrations, settings         
    - But where does document upload fit? Is it:                           
        - Admin-only (centralized document management)?                    
      - Available in multiple workspaces (PMs upload project docs, FinOps  
  upload budgets)?                                                         
      - Needs its own "Knowledge Base" or "Documents" workspace?           
  3. Cross-cutting features - Some things might be needed everywhere:      
    - Search                                                               
    - Notifications                                                        
    - Document access                                                      
    - Comments/collaboration                                               
  4. Governance overlap - Approvals might be needed in multiple workspaces 
  (PM approves task completion, FinOps approves budgets, Executive approves
   initiatives)                                                            
                                                                           
  Recommendations                                                          
                                                                           
  1. Add role flexibility - Let Firebase auth support multiple roles per   
  user with a role switcher                                                
  2. Clarify document strategy - Is it centralized (Admin) or distributed  
  (each workspace has upload)?                                             
  3. Consider a 9th workspace - "Knowledge Hub" or "Documents" if document 
  management is a major feature                                            
  4. Shared components header - Global search, notifications, quick actions
   available in all workspaces                                             
  5. Permission model - Some tabs within workspaces might have view-only vs
   edit permissions
